Why We Are Committed To Flat Rates

Providing flat rate billing, also known as fixed fee billing, introduces more certainty in your legal journey. Our business model’s success relies on quality client experiences, not drawn-out battles. We advocate for healthy relationships between all parties involved, during and after your legal engagement, and work towards results that keep your best interests focused.

Although there are few winners in family law with compromise and compassion, we find lasting resolutions. Our respect for the individual and family is not to be mistaken for weakness. Our legal team negotiates fiercely and has the depth of court and trial experience necessary to confront an unreasonable adversary.
